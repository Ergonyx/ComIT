Posted on: Feb 8, 2020

Music has been a large part of my life lately as I've used it to motivate myself, direct my behavior, and remain inspired. Being a transit user, this gives me ample opportunities to listen to music and get myself in a calm mindset before an interview, a creative flow state before brainstorming with friends, or pumped up before a workout. LOL. I don't work out.
